We at Vedant Jaiswal Foundation are working together to educate our new generation, We are working in many areas, where we take care about basic needs of children such as food, clothes, and education. We are not only accepting donation in terms of money, you can also donate your time and teach some students who are not capable of getting paid education. You can also donate your old books, clothes, food, etc.
